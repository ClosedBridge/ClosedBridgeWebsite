Frequently Asked Questions

What to expect when working with us

Do you sign NDAs?

Yes. We will sign an NDA before any confidential information is shared. We treat all client information with complete discretion and never share details about one engagement with another client.

Who owns the IP for work you develop?

You do. All intellectual property developed during an engagement is fully owned by the client. This is standard in our agreements.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Most engagements start with a scoping call to understand the problem and define deliverables. We typically work in phases — getting you to a working prototype quickly, then iterating toward a production-ready system. Scope and timeline are agreed upon before work begins.

Do you work with early-stage startups or only established companies?

Both. We've worked with early-stage teams that need to move fast and with established companies that need specialized expertise for a specific subsystem. If you have a hardware problem that needs solving, we're interested in talking.

Are you remote?

Yes, we operate fully remote with engineers distributed across US timezones. Most client communication is async-friendly, but we're happy to join calls or sync as needed.

How do I get started?

Reach out via the contact page with a brief description of your project. We'll schedule a call to discuss scope and see if we're the right fit.

Control Systems

Real-time control for demanding applications

We design and implement real-time control systems for robotics, motor drives, aerospace, and industrial applications. From classical PID to state-space and model-based control, we build algorithms that perform reliably under real hardware constraints — and we validate them in simulation before they ever touch metal.

Capabilities

  • PID, cascade, and feedforward controller design and tuning
  • State-space and model predictive control (MPC)
  • Trajectory planning and motion profiling
  • Simulation and hardware-in-the-loop (HIL) validation
  • Sensor fusion and state estimation (Kalman filters, observers)
  • Thrust vector control and attitude control systems
  • Hard real-time implementation on microcontrollers and DSPs

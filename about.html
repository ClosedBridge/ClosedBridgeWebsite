About Us

Boutique embedded engineering, built to ship

ClosedBridge LLC is a boutique engineering consulting firm specializing in embedded systems, real-time control, and robotics. We work with hardware companies, startups, and research teams to take products from early prototype all the way through to production-ready systems.

Our Mission

The hardest part of bringing a hardware product to market isn't writing the code — it's knowing which shortcuts to take on the way to a prototype and which ones will cost you later. ClosedBridge is built around one idea: get you to a working prototype fast, then carry it the rest of the way to a product you can ship with confidence.

The Team

We're a team of six engineers distributed across the United States. Our engineers bring deep hands-on experience across firmware, real-time control, motor drive, embedded Linux, hardware design, and computer vision. We stay small by design — every engagement gets senior-level attention.

How We Work

  • We embed with your team rather than working in isolation
  • All intellectual property developed for you stays yours
  • We sign NDAs and treat your information with full discretion
  • We operate fully remote, across US timezones, with async-friendly processes
